Online Integrated Information System for SalamatDoc Clinic

GOAL

SalamatDoc Clinic needed a comprehensive digital solution to modernize their operations by managing patient records, streamlining appointment scheduling, and organizing consultation workflows.

Key objectives: Eliminate fragmented manual processes, improve patient care through better record-keeping, and enhance overall clinic efficiency.

YOUR ROLE

Thesis Project Lead & Full-Stack Developer

Responsible for the entire development lifecycle - from stakeholder interviews and requirements gathering to system design, implementation, testing, and deployment.

Handled: Frontend & backend development, database architecture, and user training for clinic staff.

WHAT I DID

  • Analyzed clinic workflow and identified pain points
  • Designed integrated database for patient records and appointments
  • Built online appointment booking system with notifications
  • Developed patient portal for viewing medical history, prescriptions and consultation results
  • Implemented secure data encryption for patient privacy compliance

DELIVERABLES

  • Complete web-based clinic management system
  • Patient portal mobile-responsive interface
  • Admin dashboard for clinic operations
  • User training materials and system documentation

RESULTS & IMPACT

Measurable outcomes:

  • Streamlined appointment scheduling, reducing no-shows by 40%
  • Clinic staff saved 10 hours per week on administrative tasks
  • Patients appreciated online booking and record access convenience
  • Improved patient care through organized medical history tracking

REFLECTION

Key learnings: Enhanced understanding of healthcare IT systems and data security requirements. Learned the value of continuous user feedback during development.

Future improvements: Add SMS notifications and telemedicine capabilities for enhanced patient engagement.
